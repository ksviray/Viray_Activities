About

The story revolves around a highschool detective named Kudo Shinichi who was forced to drink a age shrinking pill by an evil organization he accidentally saw in the middle of a drug trade. Afterwards, He ended up living with her children friend Mouri Ran and her father Mouri Kogoro who happens to be a detective like him but not as bright. Since the home of her friend Ran happens to be a detective agency at the same time, he currently stays there solving crimes undercover while also finding the mysterious organization that made him like this.
